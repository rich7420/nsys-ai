AI capabilities and controls
Supported UI actions
  • navigate_to_kernel(target_name): select and highlight a kernel.
  • zoom_to_time_range(start_s, end_s): zoom timeline to a time window.
  • fit_nvtx_range(...): fit an NVTX range to full width (by name or start/end).
How to control it
  • Natural language: ask directly, e.g. “go to kernel X”.
  • Deterministic: ask explicitly for an action by name and exact target.
